Inner Oasis

A healing station for your inner world

Life often feels like a long road you are expected to keep running on, with roles, responsibilities, and expectations pulling at you from every direction.

You have learned to keep going, to be responsible, understanding, accommodating. To tell yourself, "just a little longer."

But when everything finally slows down, the exhaustion you have been holding back rises: the thoughts you cannot outrun, the ache beneath them, the loneliness and small hurts you never had room to feel.

If you are looking for a place where you do not have to be strong, do not have to explain, do not have to perform, a place to pause, to breathe, to gather yourself, this is the space I have prepared for you.

A small inner station, where you can move at your own pace and continue forward with a heart that can breathe again.

Who I Am

Hello, I'm Annie.

I am a Licensed Professional Counselor in Oregon, offering bilingual telehealth psychotherapy in English, Mandarin, and Cantonese.

I work with adults who carry quiet heaviness, often shaped by emotional overwhelm, people-pleasing patterns, OCD, trauma responses, or the pressure of navigating life between cultures.

My work is gentle, steady, and collaborative. We move at a pace that respects your nervous system and the ways you have learned to survive.

Practice Focus

  • Emotional overwhelm and life transitions
  • People-pleasing and relational strain
  • OCD, trauma responses, and cultural identity stress
